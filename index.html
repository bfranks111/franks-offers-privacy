This Privacy Policy describes how Franks Financial LLC (operating as Franks Offers) ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you submit a lead form on our Facebook advertisements or otherwise contact us about selling your property.

1. Who We Are

Franks Financial LLC is a real estate investment company that purchases residential properties using creative financing structures (including seller-financing arrangements). We operate primarily in Texas and Florida.

2. Information We Collect

When you submit a Facebook Lead Form or otherwise inquire about selling your property, we collect:

  • Contact information — your name, email address, and phone number (auto-filled from your Facebook profile or entered manually)
  • Property information — the address of the property you are considering selling
  • Pricing information — the price you are hoping to receive for your property
  • Selling context — your reason for considering a sale (e.g., relocating, mortgage burden, inheritance, tired landlord)
  • Preferred contact times — your preferred windows for a follow-up call

We do not collect Social Security numbers, financial account numbers, or other sensitive identifiers through our lead forms.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Contact you about your property and discuss a potential purchase
  • Prepare and present an offer or letter of intent
  • Conduct due diligence on the property (e.g., reviewing public records and comparable sales)
  • Communicate with you throughout any resulting transaction
  • Maintain records of our interactions for legal and operational purposes

We do not sell your information, share it with marketing companies, or use it to send unsolicited promotional content unrelated to your property inquiry.

4. Who We Share Information With

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our business (e.g., customer relationship management software, email and SMS infrastructure providers acting as data processors on our behalf)
  • Title companies, escrow agents, and closing attorneys if your property moves into a transaction
  • Our attorneys, accountants, and advisors as reasonably necessary
  • Government authorities or courts if legally required (e.g., subpoena, court order)

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

4a. SMS / Text Messaging Program

We operate an SMS / text-messaging program for two-way communication about real estate transactions. The terms below apply specifically to our text-messaging program and are required disclosures under U.S. carrier regulations (CTIA Best Practices and TCR / The Campaign Registry).

How you opt in. You opt into receiving SMS messages from Franks Financial LLC by one of the following actions, all of which constitute your express consent:

  • Submitting a Facebook Lead Form, our Calendly booking page, or any web form on our property where the form clearly indicates that providing your phone number authorizes us to call or text you about your inquiry.
  • Replying to one of our outbound emails (sent from buy@franksoffers.com, ben@franksoffers.com, or ben@franksdeals.com) and either expressing interest in being contacted, asking us to call/text, or providing your phone number for follow-up.
  • Sending an SMS to our business number (213) 602-8555 first.

What you can expect. Messages are one-to-one and relate to a specific property inquiry, offer, or transaction. Message frequency varies based on the conversation — typically zero to a few messages per week per active deal.

Carrier disclosure. Message and data rates may apply. Msg & data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

How to stop. Reply STOP to any text message at any time to opt out. You may also reply STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT. We will send you one confirmation message acknowledging your opt-out, after which you will receive no further SMS from us. To resubscribe, reply START.

How to get help. Reply HELP to any text message for assistance, or email support@franksdeals.com, or call (213) 602-8555.

No third-party sharing of mobile information. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. This includes your phone number, SMS opt-in status, and message content — none of these are shared, sold, rented, or transferred to any third parties or affiliates for marketing, advertising, or any other promotional purpose. Mobile information sharing is limited solely to the SMS infrastructure provider (Twilio Inc.) acting as a data processor under contract to deliver the messages you have requested.

For full SMS terms, see our SMS Terms & Conditions.
